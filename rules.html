Game Rules



Goal:

Score the most points by handling and placing the following engine components that will be on the playing field: fans, cores, and turbines. These components are placed into engines.

In game rules

Before the game...

- Blue team vs yellow team: 2 robots on each team.
- 5 minutes per heat.


Each engine component can come in blue, yellow, or red. Each team uses the parts specific to their team color.

6 engines, each can be completed with one fan, one core, one turbine

Robots can only carry one component at a time. Cannot carry the other team's color: only their team's color and the red ones.

Red parts: unserviceable. If the component that falls out of an engine is a red part, it can be repaired in the repair station. Each team has three repair stations that can turn red parts into repaired parts of their color.
