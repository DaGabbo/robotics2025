GOAL: Place your game pieces in 1 of the 9 stations. You can control stations by completing certain conditions! If you control 3 stations in the same row, column, or diagonal, you get a multiplier on the points you score in those stations!

1. Two starting zones

This year, robots can start in 1 of 2 starting zones of their colour!

2. Scoring the Points

Each game piece placed in a station earns points. A curling rink is at the heart of the action!

3. Stations

The robots will be able to try and control up to 9 stations on the playing field!

4. Bonus Points

Awarded if you own three stations in the same line

5. Time

5 minutes per heat!

The Bat Code

Be kind, respect others, help others when needed
